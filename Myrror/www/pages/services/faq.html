Frequently Asked Questions

Can the myrror be moved?

Yes, some people get scared that the myrror will lose it’s data of the person who’s using it. There’s no need to scan and import yourself all over again.

How long does the delivery take?

The moment you push on the place order button, we immediately send the information of the order to the companies we’re affiliated with. They usually ship between 1-3 days, depends on your order. After it has been shipped you will receive it in 24-hours.
